How it works

Four steps to interview-ready

MockDebut takes your CV and a job posting URL and turns them into a personalized mock interview session.

1

Upload your CV & JD

Paste your CV text or upload a PDF, then add the job description URL or text.

2

Choose your persona

Pick a Strict Technical Lead, Friendly HR, or Culture-Focused CEO as your interviewer.

3

Do the mock interview

Answer AI-generated questions tailored to your profile. STAR hints guide behavioral answers.

4

Review your report

Get a full performance report with radar chart, strengths, and specific areas to improve.
